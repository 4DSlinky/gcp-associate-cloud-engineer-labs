# Risk Management

## Definitions

- **Threat** — something that *could* cause harm
- **Vulnerability** — a weakness that a threat could exploit
- **Risk** — the likelihood and impact of a threat exploiting a vulnerability
- **Control** — something that reduces likelihood or impact

## Risk treatment options

- **Mitigate** — reduce likelihood or impact with controls
- **Transfer** — push risk to someone else (insurance, vendor)
- **Avoid** — don't do the risky activity
- **Accept** — document and live with it

## Frameworks worth knowing

- NIST CSF
- ISO/IEC 27001
- CIS Controls
- Cloud-specific: CIS GCP Benchmark
